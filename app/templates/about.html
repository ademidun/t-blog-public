About t-blog

by Tomiwa

August 27, 2016 7:37pm

T-blog is a blogging application developed in python using the flask microframework and based on Miguel Grinberg's tutorial, (thanks Miguel!).This blog is a prototype created by Atila Tech for clients that wish to have an internal social media service for their company. Check out my short essay for my thoughts about this application.

How to use t-blog

Ideally, users post (and cite) various quotes that they find interesting on the internet or excerpts from interesting books or articles they read. Please cite all sources.

  1. Create a Yahoo or AOL account.
  2. Follow any of the following accounts
    • galbraith
    • dup
    • keynes
  3. Start blogging!
